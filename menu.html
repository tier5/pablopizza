Having created hundreds of different pizzas, Paul and Kaci are happy to say that almost anything you put on a pizza will be tasty. There have been a few exceptions, but you won't find those on the menu!

Pablo's Pizza has continued to enhance their menu, adding more pizzas, salads, soup and sandwiches. The original menu had 13 pizzas and that was it. The current menu has 26 pizzas, plus 7 salads, desserts and sandwiches. We always accept suggestions for a rotating pizza of the week, allowing customers to become "pizza maestros."

Pablo's Pizza emphasizes using quality ingredients always. We believe that you get what you pay for with pizza. If someone is selling cheap pizza, they are cutting corners on the ingredient quality and that is reflected in the final product.

PiZZA

Pizzas come in 8", 12" and 18" sizes. We're happy to make half-n-half pizzas. We charge for the more expensive half. Gluten free dough available in 8” and 12” **Unfortunately, we cannot guarantee the dough hasn't come into contact with flour.
