Pablo's Pizza was inspired by Pablo Picasso who forever changed the art world with his revolutionary creations. We hope for the same sort of impact on your taste buds. Once subjected to the melange of flavors and quality ingredients, you will be forever ensnared, desperately awaiting the next meal at Pablos's Pizza.


PHiLOSOPHY

We feel that eating pizza should be intertwined with the best parts of life: exploring, learning and loving. We have confidence that our establishment will meet our customer's needs on a physical, emotional, and spiritual level. No person' s life is ordinary. We hope that our pizza will become part of every extra-ordinary life that graces our world.


LOCATiON

Pablo's Pizza Grand Junction is the original Pablo's and is situated in a historical building amidst the quaint shops, hotels and other restaurants on the tree-lined Main Street. Choose from dozens of specialty pizzas, create your own masterpiece or choose a classic.


OWNER'S

Paul and Kaci Knaysi opened Pablo's Pizza in October 2000, after 3 years of planning and research. Paul and Kaci had been working as a medical social worker and a physical therapist respectively. Tiring of the medical field, they decided to go into business for themselves.

After exploring different business options, they settled on pizza, as they felt that it would be fun to own a pizzeria and allow for creativity. Of course it helps that almost everyone loves pizza! Their philosophy has been to be as extraordinary as possible. They firmly believe that if you think outside the box, the world is a lot more interesting.
